PEST AND DISEASE ADVISORY FOR VIDARBHA CROPS
Source: ICAR (Indian Council of Agricultural Research), Vikaspedia, KVK Nagpur/Akola

COTTON PEST MANAGEMENT (Vidarbha - Main crop)

1. PINK BOLLWORM (Pectinophora gossypiella) - गुलाबी बोंड अळी
Identification:
- Pink colored larvae inside cotton bolls
- Flower petals twisted like 'rosette'
- Bolls fail to open properly, seeds stick together
- Visible in August-October

Control measures:
- Use Bt cotton varieties (approved varieties)
- Install pheromone traps: 5 traps per acre, replace lure every 21 days
- Spray Chlorpyrifos 20 EC @ 2.5 ml/litre OR Profenophos 50 EC @ 2 ml/litre
- Destroy crop residue after harvest
- Report to nearest KVK or agriculture department
Helpline: KVK Nagpur: 0712-2500477

2. WHITEFLY (Bemisia tabaci) - पांढरी माशी
Identification:
- Tiny white insects under leaves
- Leaves turn yellow, curl downward
- Transmits Cotton Leaf Curl Virus

Control measures:
- Spray Imidacloprid 17.8 SL @ 0.5 ml/litre
- Use yellow sticky traps
- Avoid excessive nitrogen fertilizer
- Spray in evening hours only

3. APHIDS (Aphis gossypii) - माव
Identification:
- Tiny green/black insects in colonies on tender shoots
- Sticky honeydew on leaves

Control:
- Spray Dimethoate 30 EC @ 2 ml/litre
- Encourage natural predators (ladybird beetles)

SOYBEAN PEST MANAGEMENT (Vidarbha - Second major crop)

1. STEM FLY (Melanagromyza sojae)
Identification:
- Yellowing of leaves in seedling stage
- Stem shows tunneling when cut open
- Wilting and death of seedlings (deadheart)

Control:
- Seed treatment: Thiamethoxam 30 FS @ 10 ml/kg seed before sowing
- Spray Dimethoate 30 EC @ 2 ml/litre at 15 days after emergence

2. GIRDLE BEETLE
Identification:
- Pods and stems show girdling marks
- Branches break at girdle point

Control:
- Spray Chlorantraniliprole 18.5 SC @ 0.3 ml/litre
- Collect and destroy fallen branches

3. YELLOW MOSAIC VIRUS
Identification:
- Yellow patches on leaves
- Spread by whitefly

Control:
- No direct cure - control whitefly vector
- Remove and destroy infected plants immediately
- Spray Imidacloprid to control whitefly

---

TUR/ARHAR PEST MANAGEMENT

1. POD BORER (Helicoverpa armigera)
Identification:
- Larvae bore into pods and eat seeds
- Visible in October-December (flowering-podding stage)

Control:
- Install pheromone traps: 5 per acre
- Spray NPV (Nuclear Polyhedrosis Virus) @ 250 LE/acre
- Chemical: Indoxacarb 15.8 EC @ 0.7 ml/litre

---

GENERAL ADVISORY:
- Always spray in evening (5 PM - 7 PM) to protect bees
- Use proper protective gear when spraying
- Follow label instructions for dosage
- For accurate diagnosis, send sample to KVK

COMMON FARMER QUESTIONS:

Q: Maza cotton la pink bollworm lagla, kay karu? (Pink bollworm attacked my cotton, what to do?)
A: Pheromone saptade laava - per acre 5. Chlorpyrifos 20 EC 2.5 ml prati litre paanyat mison favarava. Sandhyakali 5 nanter favara. KVK Nagpur la call kara: 0712-2500477.

Q: Soybean la piwala padala, karan kay? (Soybean is turning yellow, what is the reason?)
A: Doh karan aasat - stem fly kiva yellow mosaic virus. Kond kapat pahaa - middle bhag hollow asel tar stem fly. Pattyavar piwale thebbe asel tar virus. Whitefly control karaychi aahe - Imidacloprid 0.5 ml/litre favara.

Q: Tur la aळी lagali, kay karu? (Worm attack on tur, what to do?)
A: He pod borer ahe. Pheromone saptade laava. Indoxacarb 15.8 EC 0.7 ml prati litre favara. Sandhyakali favara kara.
