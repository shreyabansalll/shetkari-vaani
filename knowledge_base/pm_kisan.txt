PM-KISAN SAMMAN NIDHI YOJANA
Source: pmkisan.gov.in (Official Government of India Portal)

WHAT IS PM-KISAN?
PM Kisan Samman Nidhi is a central government scheme that provides direct income support of Rs. 6,000 per year to all eligible farmer families across India. Money is transferred directly to bank accounts in 3 equal installments of Rs. 2,000 each.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE?
- All landholding farmer families with cultivable land
- Both small and marginal farmers
- Tenant farmers are NOT eligible (land must be in farmer's name)
- Family = husband, wife, and minor children

WHO IS NOT ELIGIBLE?
- Institutional landholders
- Farmer families where any member is/was an income tax payer
- Government employees (current or retired)
- Professionals like doctors, engineers, lawyers, CAs
- Persons with monthly pension above Rs. 10,000

BENEFIT:
- Rs. 6,000 per year
- Paid in 3 installments of Rs. 2,000 each
- Installment schedule: April-July, August-November, December-March
- Direct Bank Transfer (DBT) to Aadhaar-linked bank account

HOW TO REGISTER:
1. Visit pmkisan.gov.in
2. Click "New Farmer Registration"
3. Enter Aadhaar number and mobile number
4. Fill land details (survey number, area, crop)
5. Submit and verify via OTP
OR
Visit nearest CSC (Common Service Centre) with documents

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Aadhaar card
- Bank passbook (account linked to Aadhaar)
- Land records (7/12 utara / RoR)
- Mobile number linked to Aadhaar

HOW TO CHECK STATUS:
- Visit pmkisan.gov.in → "Beneficiary Status"
- Enter Aadhaar number or mobile number or bank account number
- Helpline: 155261 or 1800-115-526 (Toll Free)

EKYC IS MANDATORY:
All PM-Kisan beneficiaries must complete eKYC
- Online: pmkisan.gov.in → eKYC option (OTP via Aadhaar-linked mobile)
- Offline: Visit nearest CSC centre with Aadhaar card
- Without eKYC, installment may be stopped

COMMON FARMER QUESTIONS:

Q: PM Kisan yojana sathi mi eligible aahe ka? (Am I eligible for PM Kisan?)
A: Tumchi jamin tumchya navavar asel, income tax bharit nasat, ani sarkaari naukri nasel tar PM Kisan sathi eligible aahat. Varshi Rs. 6,000 teen hapta madhe miltat.

Q: PM Kisan madhye register karaycha kasa? (How to register for PM Kisan?)
A: pmkisan.gov.in var jaa kiva najik CSC center var jaa. Aadhaar, 7/12 utara, ani Aadhaar linked bank account ghewa.

Q: Paise kab aayenge PM Kisan ke? (When will PM Kisan money come?)
A: Saal mein 3 baar Rs. 2,000-2,000 aate hain. April-July, August-November, aur December-March mein. Status check karo pmkisan.gov.in pe ya 155261 pe call karo.

Q: eKYC karaychi kashi? (How to do eKYC?)
A: pmkisan.gov.in var eKYC option ahe. Aadhaar linked mobile number ne OTP through kara. Kiva CSC center var Aadhaar ghetun jaa.
