GOVERNMENT SCHEMES FOR VIDARBHA FARMERS
Source: myscheme.gov.in, maharashtra.gov.in, DAC&FW

1. PM-KISAN SAMMAN NIDHI
- Benefit: Rs. 6,000 per year (3 installments of Rs. 2,000)
- Eligible: All landowner farmer families
- Register: pmkisan.gov.in or nearest CSC
- Helpline: 155261

2. PMFBY - CROP INSURANCE
- Benefit: Compensation for crop loss due to natural calamities, pests, disease
- Premium: Only 2% for Kharif crops
- Claim: Call 1800-180-1551 within 72 hours of damage
- Register: pmfby.gov.in or bank or CSC

3. KISAN CREDIT CARD (KCC)
- Benefit: Short-term crop loan at 4% interest
- Limit: Up to Rs. 3 lakh
- Apply: Any nationalized bank with Aadhaar and land records
- Helpline: 1800-200-3838 (NABARD)

4. SOIL HEALTH CARD SCHEME
- Benefit: Free soil testing and fertilizer recommendation
- How: Register at nearest agriculture office
- Validity: 3 years
- Maharashtra Helpline: 1800-233-4000

5. PM KUSUM YOJANA (Solar Pump)
- Benefit: Solar irrigation pump with 60-90% subsidy
- For: Farmers without grid electricity connection
- Apply: Maharashtra Energy Development Agency (MEDA)
- Website: mahaurja.com

6. MAHATMA PHULE KARJ MAFI YOJANA (Maharashtra)
- Benefit: Loan waiver up to Rs. 2 lakh for eligible farmers
- Status check: Online portal or bank

7. PRADHAN MANTRI KRISHI SINCHAI YOJANA (PMKSY)
- Benefit: Drip/sprinkler irrigation subsidy (55-75% subsidy for small farmers)
- Apply: District agriculture office
- Helpline: Maharashtra irrigation dept: 1800-233-0033

8. NATIONAL FOOD SECURITY MISSION (NFSM)
- Benefit: Free/subsidized seeds and inputs for pulses and oilseeds
- For: Tur, soybean, groundnut farmers
- Apply: Nearest Block Agriculture Office (BDO)

9. RASHTRIYA KRISHI VIKAS YOJANA (RKVY)
- Benefit: Infrastructure support, FPO formation assistance
- For: Groups of farmers and FPOs

COMMON QUESTIONS:

Q: Shetkaryasathi kuthkuthly sarkaari yojana aahet? (What government schemes are available for farmers?)
A: Mukhya yojana: PM-KISAN (Rs. 6,000/year), PMFBY crop insurance (2% premium), KCC loan (4% interest), Soil Health Card (free), PM KUSUM solar pump (60-90% subsidy). Maharashtra Agriculture helpline: 1800-233-4000.

Q: Drip irrigation sathi subsidy milte ka? (Is there subsidy for drip irrigation?)
A: Ho! PMKSY yojane antargat small farmers la 75% paryant subsidy milte drip/sprinkler irrigation sathi. District agriculture office la sampark kara. Maharashtra helpline: 1800-233-0033.

Q: Solar pump sathi apply karaycha kasa? (How to apply for solar pump?)
A: PM KUSUM yojana antargat 60-90% subsidy ahe. mahaurja.com var apply kara kiva najik MEDA office la jaa.
