WEATHER ADVISORY AND GENERAL CROP GUIDANCE FOR VIDARBHA
Source: IMD (India Meteorological Department), ICAR, KVK Nagpur

VIDARBHA REGION - KEY CROPS AND CALENDAR:

COTTON (Kharif):
- Sowing: June - July (after monsoon onset)
- Harvesting: October - January
- Key districts: Yavatmal, Wardha, Nagpur, Akola, Amravati, Buldhana

SOYBEAN (Kharif):
- Sowing: June - July
- Harvesting: September - October
- Key districts: Nagpur, Wardha, Amravati, Yavatmal

TUR/ARHAR (Kharif):
- Sowing: June - July
- Harvesting: December - February
- Key districts: Yavatmal, Wardha, Nagpur

ORANGE (Mandarin - Nagpur Santra):
- Harvesting: November - January (Ambia bahar)
- Nagpur orange is GI tagged product
- Key districts: Nagpur, Wardha, Amravati

GENERAL FARMING TIPS FOR VIDARBHA:

Water Conservation:
- Use drip irrigation for cotton - saves 40% water
- Mulching reduces moisture loss

Fertilizer Best Practices:
- Cotton: NPK 100:50:50 kg per hectare
- Soybean: Rhizobium seed treatment before sowing reduces N fertilizer need
- Use soil test results from KVK for accurate fertilizer dosage

COMMON QUESTIONS:

Q: KVK madhe kasa jaayche? (How to go to KVK?)
A: Tumchya district chya KVK la call kara - Nagpur: 0712-2500477, Yavatmal: 07232-244722, Amravati: 0721-2668430. Te free mati parikshan ani pik salya detet.

Q: Vidarbhyat cotton la kiti paani lagte? (How much water does cotton need in Vidarbha?)
A: Cotton la drip irrigation madhe 500-600 mm paani lagte. Paaosavar aadharit cotton la 700-800 mm lagte. Drip irrigation sathi PM KUSUM madhe 75% subsidy milte.

Q: Nagpur santrya sathi kuthali yojana ahe? (What scheme is available for Nagpur orange?)
A: Nagpur Santra GI tagged product ahe. NABARD kadun FPO (Farmer Producer Organisation) sathi support milte. Horticulture department, Nagpur: 0712-2563308 la sampark kara.
