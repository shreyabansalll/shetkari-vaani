KISAN CREDIT CARD (KCC) SCHEME
Source: nabard.org and Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

WHAT IS KISAN CREDIT CARD?
Kisan Credit Card (KCC) is a government scheme that provides farmers with affordable short-term credit for agricultural needs. It works like a credit card/overdraft facility specifically for farming expenses.

BENEFITS:
- Credit limit based on land holdings and crop requirements
- Interest rate: 7% per annum (effective rate 4% with government interest subvention)
- Flexible repayment linked to crop harvest season
- Can be used for seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, farm equipment
- Also covers post-harvest expenses and household needs
- Personal accident insurance coverage of Rs. 50,000 included
- No collateral required up to Rs. 1.6 lakh

CREDIT LIMIT CALCULATION:
- Based on: crop area x scale of finance per crop
- Short-term credit: Up to Rs. 3 lakh at 4% effective interest
- Above Rs. 3 lakh: Market rate of interest applies

WHO IS ELIGIBLE?
- Individual/Joint borrowers who are owner cultivators
- Tenant farmers, oral lessees, sharecroppers
- SHG (Self Help Groups) members
- Farmers engaged in allied activities (animal husbandry, fisheries)

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Application form (from bank)
- Aadhaar card
- PAN card (if loan above Rs. 50,000)
- Land records (7/12 extract)
- Passport size photograph
- Address proof

HOW TO APPLY:
1. Visit nearest bank branch (any nationalized bank, cooperative bank, or RRB)
2. Collect KCC application form
3. Submit with land documents and Aadhaar
4. Bank processes within 2 weeks
5. KCC card issued — use at any ATM or bank branch

IMPORTANT: PM-KISAN beneficiaries are automatically given KCC facility. Apply at bank with PM-KISAN registration proof.

REPAYMENT:
- Kharif crops: Repay by March 31
- Rabi crops: Repay by September 30
- Timely repayment ensures renewal and continued low interest rate

HELPLINE:
- NABARD: 1800-200-3838 (Toll Free)
- Your nearest bank branch

COMMON FARMER QUESTIONS:

Q: Kisan Credit Card kadhaycha kasa? (How to get Kisan Credit Card?)
A: Najik bank la jaa - SBI, Bank of Maharashtra, kiva koi sarkaari bank. KCC application form bhara, Aadhaar ani 7/12 utara ghewa. 2 aadhyat card milel. 4% vyajdar ne karz milel.

Q: KCC madhe kiiti karz milel? (How much loan in KCC?)
A: Tumchya jaminiche maap ani pikaanusaar. Rs. 3 lakh paryant 4% vyajdar. Biyane, khate, keetnashak, sarvansathi vaparu shakat.

Q: KCC sathi kiti vyaj bharave lagte? (How much interest to pay for KCC?)
A: Sarkar subvention dil tar effective interest 4% per year ahe. Rs. 3 lakh paryant. Kharif pikasathi March 31 aadhi paisa para kara.
