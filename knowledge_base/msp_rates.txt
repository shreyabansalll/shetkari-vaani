MSP - MINIMUM SUPPORT PRICE FOR VIDARBHA CROPS
Source: Cabinet approval via CACP (Commission for Agricultural Costs and Prices)
Season: Kharif Marketing Season 2024-25 (approved June 19, 2024)

WHAT IS MSP?
Minimum Support Price (MSP) is the guaranteed price the Government of India pays farmers for their crops. If market prices fall below MSP, the government procures the crop at MSP to protect farmers from losses.

KHARIF 2024-25 MSP RATES (Vidarbha key crops):

COTTON:
- Cotton Medium Staple: Rs. 7,121 per quintal
- Cotton Long Staple: Rs. 7,521 per quintal
- 1 quintal = 100 kg
- Increase from last year: Rs. 501 per quintal

SOYBEAN:
- Soybean (Yellow): Rs. 4,892 per quintal
- Increase from last year: Rs. 292 per quintal
- Cost of production (C2): Rs. 4,291 per quintal
- Farmer margin over cost: 50%+

TUR/ARHAR (Pigeon Pea):
- Tur/Arhar: Rs. 7,550 per quintal
- Increase from last year: Rs. 550 per quintal (highest among pulses)
- Cost of production (C2): Rs. 6,504 per quintal
- Farmer margin over cost: 59%

MOONG (Green Gram):
- Moong: Rs. 8,682 per quintal

URAD (Black Gram):
- Urad: Rs. 7,400 per quintal

GROUNDNUT:
- Groundnut: Rs. 6,783 per quintal

WHERE TO SELL AT MSP?
- Cotton: Cotton Corporation of India (CCI) procurement centres
- Soybean/Tur: Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board (MSAMB) mandis
- Check nearest mandi: agmarknet.nic.in

HOW TO GET MSP FOR YOUR CROP?
1. Register on PM-KISAN portal or nearest mandi
2. Bring crop to government procurement centre during procurement season
3. Payment directly to bank account (DBT) within 3 working days

COMMON FARMER QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS:

Q: Cotton la aaj MSP kiiti ahe? (What is today's MSP for cotton?)
A: 2024-25 sathi cotton (medium staple) la MSP Rs. 7,121 prati quintal ahe. Long staple cotton la Rs. 7,521 prati quintal ahe.

Q: Soybean la MSP kiiti milel? (How much MSP will I get for soybean?)
A: 2024-25 sathi soybean la MSP Rs. 4,892 prati quintal ahe. He sarkarni June 2024 maadhye jaahir kele.

Q: Tur la MSP kay ahe? (What is MSP for tur?)
A: 2024-25 sathi tur/arhar la MSP Rs. 7,550 prati quintal ahe. Ya veli sarkarni Rs. 550 vadhavle - sarv pulses madhye sajaast vaadh.

Q: MSP var pik kase vikayche? (How to sell crop at MSP?)
A: Najik sarkaari mandi kiva CCI center var jaa. PM-KISAN portal var register kara. Crop aanla ki 3 diwsaat bank account madhe paise yetil.
